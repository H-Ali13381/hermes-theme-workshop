## Category : Category

`import Quickshell.Services.SystemTray `
See [StatusNotifierItem.category ](/docs/v0.3.0/types/Quickshell.Services.SystemTray/StatusNotifierItem#category).

## Variants

* SystemServices

System services such as IMEs or disk indexing.

* ApplicationStatus

The fallback category for general applications or anything that does not fit into a different category.

* Communications

Communication related applications such as instant messengers or email clients.

* Hardware

Hardware controls like battery indicators or volume control.
