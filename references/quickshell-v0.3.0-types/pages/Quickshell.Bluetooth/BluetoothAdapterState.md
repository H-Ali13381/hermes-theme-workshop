## Variants

* Enabling

The adapter is transitioning from off to on.

* Disabling

The adapter is transitioning from on to off.

* Disabled

The adapter is powered off.

* Enabled

The adapter is powered on.

* Blocked

The adapter is blocked by rfkill.
